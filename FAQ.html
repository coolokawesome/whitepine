How can I book an appointment?

To book an appointment at our tattoo studio, you can call us or visit our website and fill out the booking form. Once we receive your request, we will get in touch with you to confirm the date and time of your appointment.

Do you take walk-ins?

Yes, we do take walk-ins. However, we recommend that you call ahead to check our availability or book an appointment in advance to avoid any disappointment.

Does a tattoo hurt?

Tattooing involves piercing the skin with a needle, so there will be some level of discomfort or pain. However, the amount of pain varies depending on the individual's pain tolerance and the location of the tattoo. Our experienced artists will do their best to make you as comfortable as possible during the process.

How do I prepare for my tattoo appointment?

To prepare for your tattoo appointment, make sure to get a good night's sleep, stay hydrated, and eat a meal beforehand. Avoid drinking alcohol or taking blood-thinning medication. Also, wear loose clothing that allows easy access to the area you want tattooed.

Do you take walk-ins?

Yes, we do take walk-ins. However, we recommend that you call ahead to check our availability or book an appointment in advance to avoid any disappointment.

How long will my tattoo take to heal?

The healing time for a tattoo varies depending on the size, location, and complexity of the design. Generally, it takes 2-3 weeks for the outer layer of the skin to heal, but the complete healing process can take up to 4-6 weeks.

Can I bring a design or picture for my tattoo?

Yes, you can bring your own design or picture to your tattoo appointment. Our artists will work with you to make sure your design is the best it can be and fits well in the chosen location.

What is the minimum age requirement for getting a tattoo?

The minimum age to get a tattoo is 18 years old. If accompanied by an immediate family member/guardian, this age is 16. 2 forms of valid identifiation for both parties will be required.
